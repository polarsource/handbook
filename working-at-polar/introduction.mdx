- Flexible hours that work for your life

## Our Communication Style

### Async First
- Document decisions and context
- Use threads for discussion
- Over-communicate rather than assume

### Direct but Kind
- Clear feedback that helps people grow
- Assume positive intent
- Address issues quickly before they grow
- Celebrate wins and learn from failures

### Inclusive
- Everyone's voice matters regardless of role or tenure
- Multiple perspectives make better decisions
- Create space for different working styles
- Support each other's success

## Continuous Improvement

We regularly evolve how we work based on:
- Feedback
- What we learn from our users
- Industry best practices
- Individual needs and preferences

This handbook is a living document that reflects how we actually work, not just how we aspire to work.
